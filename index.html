ABOUT ME

Hi and welcome to my page. My name is Fernando Gaol and I am based in Provo, UT. My story as a developer started when I was 13 and I decided to build a game. I was able to pull it off by watching tutorials, but I had no idea how that code worked. From there on, I tried building more complex games, but my abilities were limited. I failed multiple times so I decided to learn more in depth how to code. However, when I was searching for game development on the web, I also found web development. For some reason, web development caught my attention the most so I started taking some courses on Code Academy such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I started coding for hours every day so that I could get better at what I like to do the most.

From that point on, I knew that I wanted to code and that it would be a very challenging goal to achieve. However, the more I challenge myself, the more I learn and accomplish things. I also realized that with Web Development I could see what I was building in real time. I would see the style and the site taking form within minutes and that was something that I had not yet experienced because prior to HTML and CSS I was only doing backend in C# and C++. .

Apart from coding, I like to play soccer, volleyball, and play games on the computer such as League of Legends, Call of Duty, and more. I also like learning new languages and spending time with friends and family, even though I spend most of my days on the computer. Other than that, I am married and I don't have any kids yet.
