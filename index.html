Hi all. My name is Fernando Gaol and I am based in Provo, UT. My story as a developer started when I was 13, and I decided to build a first-person shooting game. I was able to pull it off by watching tutorials, but I had no idea how that code worked. From there on, I tried building more complex games, but my abilities were limited. I failed multiple times so I decided to learn more in-depth on how to code. However, when I was searching for game development courses on the web, I also found web development courses. Web development caught my attention the most because with Web Development I could see what I was building in real-time. I would see the style and the site taking form within minutes and that was something that I had not yet experienced. From that point on, I started taking some courses on Code Academy such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I started coding for hours every day so that I could get better at what I like to do most.

Apart from coding, I like to play soccer, volleyball, and play games on the computer such as League of Legends, Call of Duty, and more. I also like learning new languages and spending time with friends and family; even though I spend most of my days on the computer.
